# ERIKA_5.0

Personal Agricultural assistant or possible survival tool for the lone Wanderer/Adventurer.

Utilizes:
Raspberry Pi3B+(The Grovepi+ while compatable with hundreds more sensors than its rivals, appears to be incompatable with the Raspberry Pi4 for now...)

Grovepi+-

Grove Sun Sensor-To measure Ultraviolet and Infrared radiation in regards to plant growth and human safety as well as typical visible light.

Grove Barometer Bme280- for Temperature, Humidity and Air Pressure. Which can be utilized through various formulae to determine Altitude, Rain possibility and severity, Dew Point, Wet Bulb Temperature, Pesticide efficiency and Environmental Flammability.

Grove Touch Sensor- Switch Modes

Grove Soil Moisture Sensor- Soil suitability for seeding as well as a sort of Water Locator.

Grove RGB-LCD Backlight-Color coated display.
